# Four small poems

## Tuna

Silver in the deep\
a flash of muscle and brine—\
gone before the net.

## Morning

The kettle mutters\
steam fogging the window glass\
the day not yet asked.

## Old dog

Gray around the muzzle\
he dreams of a younger run—\
paws twitch in the sun.

## City rain

Neon bleeds the street\
umbrellas bump and apologize—\
the gutters keep time.
